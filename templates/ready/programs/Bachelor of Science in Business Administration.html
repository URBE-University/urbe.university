Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

This program prepares students with the necessary skills and academic knowledge for management positions in various business enterprises.

About the program

The Bachelor’s Degree in Business Administration (B.B.A.) offers a core business curriculum infused with the application of ethics and corporate social responsibility principles in a technologically savvy environment. Graduates from this program acquire the necessary skills to solve interesting and challenging issues involving the creation and exchange of goods and services, the management and development of personnel, and the efficient and socially responsible use of resources. Our goal is to produce highly versatile, receptive and knowledgeable graduates who have mastered the fundamentals of business and have a steady command of the world’s dynamic economy. URBE’s Professional BBA qualifies you for challenging careers in a wide variety of fields, many of which are management positions.

Some of the key areas covered in this program include:

Operation Management: This area deals with the planning, organizing, and controlling of the processes involved in the production and delivery of goods and services. Students will learn about operations strategy, supply chain management, and quality control.

Organizational Behavior: This field focuses on understanding human behavior within an organizational context. Topics covered may include motivation, communication, leadership, and team dynamics.

Business Law: Students will learn about the legal aspects of business operations, including contract law, intellectual property rights, and employment law.

Marketing: This area covers the principles and practices of promoting and selling products or services. Students will learn about market research, branding, advertising, and consumer behavior.

International Business: This field deals with the challenges and opportunities of conducting business in a global context. Students will learn about international trade, cultural differences, and global marketing strategies.

Accounting: This area covers the principles and practices of financial record-keeping and reporting. Students will learn about financial statements, budgeting, and taxation.

Financial: This field focuses on the management of financial resources within an organization. Topics covered may include investment analysis, financial risk management, and capital budgeting.

Information Technology: Students will learn about the use of technology in business operations, including data management, e-commerce, and cybersecurity.

Ethics and Social Responsibility: This area deals with the moral and ethical issues involved in business operations. Students will learn about corporate social responsibility, sustainability, and ethical decision-making.

Program objectives

  • Develop students' knowledge and skills in key business disciplines: The program should aim to provide a broad understanding of various business fields such as accounting, finance, marketing, management, operations, and information technology. Students should be able to apply this knowledge in practical business situations.
  • Foster critical thinking and problem-solving abilities: The program should aim to develop students' analytical and problem-solving skills, enabling them to identify and solve complex business problems. Students should be able to analyze business situations, make decisions based on sound judgment, and evaluate the effectiveness of their decisions.
  • Enhance communication and interpersonal skills: The program should aim to improve students' communication and interpersonal skills, including verbal and written communication, teamwork, and leadership. Students should be able to communicate effectively, work collaboratively with others, and lead teams to achieve business goals.
  • Promote ethical and socially responsible behavior: The program should aim to promote ethical and socially responsible behavior among students. Students should be able to understand the ethical issues involved in business operations, and develop strategies for making socially responsible decisions. They should also be able to demonstrate ethical leadership and contribute to the betterment of society.

Career opportunities

Earning a Bachelor's Degree in Mass Communication and Marketing from URBE University opens up a wide range of exciting career opportunities in the dynamic and rapidly evolving field of media and marketing. Graduates of this program can pursue a variety of roles in advertising, public relations, journalism, digital media, and market research. They may work as account executives, media planners, content creators, social media managers, public relations specialists, journalists, or market research analysts, among many other roles. They may also pursue careers in branding, event planning, media buying, or other related fields. With their comprehensive understanding of mass communication and marketing theories and techniques, ethical considerations, and the ability to utilize industry tools and techniques, graduates of this program are well-equipped to succeed in a variety of roles and to make significant contributions to the industry.

Tuition

Prospective students interested in pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Mass Communication and Marketing at URBE University should be aware of the program's tuition and fees. The total program cost for this degree is $30,450 dollars, which includes all tuition, fees, and other related expenses. The cost per credit is $250 dollars, and the program requires a total of 120 credits to complete. Students will not need to budget for textbooks, as they are provided at no additional cost. Other fees associated with the program amount to $350 dollars, and students are required to pay a one-time registration fee of $100 dollars.
