Master of Science in Instructional Design and Technology

TheMaster’s in Instructional Design and Technology is designed for students who intend to pursue careers as experts who utilize instructional resources to improve technology-based learning and performance in various environments. The program allows students to create, implement and evaluate a diverse, modern educational experience. This course was designed with working professionals in mind and offers various concentrations such as Virtual Learning, Learning Design and Technology and Instructional Design and Development.

The Instructional Design and Technology Program prepares professionals to serve a variety of audiences in diverse educational contexts in the areas of technology-based training, design, development and curriculum building. The coursework will provide students with the experience needed to effectively use technology and multimedia tools. The course connects theoretical concepts to real world applications.

Online Model

URBE University’s online platform provides students with the latest tools for collaborative virtual learning environment. Each student has the opportunity to study and apply innovative solutions for the courses; collaboration between the faculty and other students; and online resources that help the student to develop the knowledge. Our students learn doing activities in the virtual classroom as well as simulations, group encounters, attending video conferences, among other very practical activities.

Program objectives

  • URBE University’s Master of Science in Instructional Design and Technology train students to become proficient in utilizing instructional resources to improve technology-based learning and performance in various environments.
  • The Master of Science in Instructional Design and Technology Program equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to create, implement and evaluate a diverse and modern educational experience.
  • The program prepare students to serve a variety of audiences in diverse educational contexts in the areas of technology-based training, design, development and curriculum building.
  • The program also provide students with practical experience in effectively using technology and multimedia tools, and to connect theoretical concepts to real-world applications.

What you’ll learn

URBE University’s Master in Instructional Design and Technology Program encourages students to develop an investigative, creative, critical, transformative and responsible attitude towards the use and application of ICT in education and training, which favors freedom, respect, and participation in different media and technological platforms.

Tuition

Prospective students interested in pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Mass Communication and Marketing at URBE University should be aware of the program's tuition and fees. The total program cost for this degree is $13,850 dollars, which includes all tuition, fees, and other related expenses. The cost per credit is $300 dollars, and the program requires a total of 120 credits to complete. Students will not need to budget for textbooks, as they are provided at no additional cost. Other fees associated with the program amount to $350 dollars, and students are required to pay a one-time registration fee of $100 dollars.
